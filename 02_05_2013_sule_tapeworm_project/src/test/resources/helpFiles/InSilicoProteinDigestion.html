In Silico Protein Digestion

Insert a protein sequence, either in FASTA format or simply a continous sequence of amino acids, and select an enzyme to in silico digest the sequence. The maxium number of missed cleavages and lower and upper mass limits can also be selected.

All theoretical peptides are listed in the Peptides table, and the coverage is displayed in the Sequence Coverage panel to right, where the covered parts of the sequence and the currently selected peptde (in the Peptide table) are highlighted.
